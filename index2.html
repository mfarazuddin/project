Our Mission

Through Petuk Ji- Business App we intend to help our local sellers and food joint owners to increase their business by providing local seller a platform to go online and additional platform to food joints. Our lower commissions from sellers shall ensure that they would want to have business through us. Our app shall also allow you to create your own promotions and showcase items on the go. All in all, a great partnership for your business !!

BENEFITS THAT WE PROVIDE

Lowest Commission Structure

Food items need to be priced higher because food delivery partners charge a significant commission from the total sales. We believe that delivering value-for-money will help fostering long term relationships. Hence our commissions shall be significantly lower than the current market prevailing rates.

Flexibility for delivery

We allow all 3 possible options - Delivery by Restaurant / Delivery by Petuk Rider / Take away by the customer Helps provide flexibility & cost optimization to both customers and business owners

Real time reports

You can see improvements in orders & revenue over time. We shall showcase simple analysis like which category or which items are selling more or selling less, customers from which areas are ordering more or less etc.

One Time wallet balance

Used to create your business/food ads on our app, using all available promotion options – We shall handhold you through the initial few promotions till you can do it all by yourself.

Manage all orders of different delivery types in one place

Since there are three kinds of delivery (Self Pickup / Restaurant delivery / Petuk delivery), you will need to be able to classify each of them so that proper tracking is available.

Easy Menu Uploading options

Upload all your menu items at once – Changing or Updating your menu items, and feeling tensed about uploading it to the app ? Worry not.. with Petuk, you can upload all menu items in a simple process. We shall guide you how to go about it.
